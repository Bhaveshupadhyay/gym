Train like 7-time Mr. Olympia Arnold Schwarzenegger

Introduction

Today, we are going to dive into a full six-day hypertrophy program based on the Arnold Split. The Arnold Split is a highly underrated split setup for six days per week that is well-designed for beginners or individuals who respond well to low volumes. In this blog, we will provide a program walkthrough, discuss the weekly setup, and explore the pros and cons of this six-day Arnold Split program.

Program Walkthrough

Arnold Split program is a low volume program specifically designed for beginners or individuals who respond well to low volumes. The program is structured as follows:

Day 1: Chest and Back

  • Dumbbell Bench Press - 3 sets
  • T-Bar Rows - 3 sets
  • Lat Pulldowns - 3 sets
  • Cable Upright Rows - 3 sets
  • AB Training - 3 trisets

Day 2: Shoulders and Arms

  • Dumbbell Overhead Press - 3 sets
  • Dumbbell Curls - 3 sets
  • Easy Bar Skull Crushers - 3 sets
  • Lying Bicep Curls - 3 sets
  • Cable Lateral Raises - 3 sets

Day 3: Legs

  • Front Squats - 3 sets
  • Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts - 3 sets
  • Leg Presses - 3 sets
  • Calf Raises - 3 sets
  • Light Curls - 2 sets

Day 4: Chest and Back

  • Incline Bench Press - 3 sets
  • Lat Pulldowns - 3 sets
  • Cable Rows - 3 sets
  • Cable Upright Rows - 3 sets
  • AB Training - 3 trisets

Day 5: Shoulders and Arms

  • Weighted Dips - 3 sets
  • Easy Bar Preacher Curls - 3 sets
  • Rope Press Downs - 3 sets
  • Rope Hammer Curls - 3 sets
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises - 3 sets

Day 6: Legs

  • Trap Bar Deadlifts - 2 sets
  • Smith Machine Squats - 3 sets
  • Lunges - 3 sets
  • Leg Extensions - 3 sets
  • Machine Calf Raises - 3 sets

Each exercise has a specific number of sets and a target rep range. It's important to note that the program incorporates a top set back off method, which allows you to work up to a heavy set followed by lighter back-off sets. This method provides a balance between heavy strength work and accumulating volume.

Weekly Setup

  • Day 1: Chest and Back
  • Day 2: Shoulders and Arms
  • Day 3: Legs
  • Day 4: Chest and Back
  • Day 5: Shoulders and Arms
  • Day 6: Legs
  • Day 7: Rest

This ordering of days helps to distribute fatigue and optimize training performance. By interspersing shoulder and arm days between leg and chest/back days, it allows for better fatigue management. Additionally, having a rest day after the toughest leg day ensures optimal recovery.

Pros and Cons

Let's explore the pros and cons of the six-day Arnold Split program:

Pros

  • Training shoulders and arms when they're fresh: The dedicated shoulder and arm days in the program allow for better performance and volume.
  • High frequency of shoulder and arm training: By indirectly training biceps and triceps in chest and back workouts, the program effectively targets these muscle groups four times per week.
  • High frequency of pressing: The program emphasizes pressing movements, which can be beneficial for developing pressing strength.
  • Superset-friendly: The Arnold Split works well with supersets, especially for antagonist muscle groups like chest and back, biceps and triceps. This approach saves time without sacrificing productivity.

Cons

  • Shoulders coming after chest: This is a common critique of the Arnold Split. However, exercise modifications and careful exercise selection can help mitigate any potential interference.
  • Sub-optimal fatigue distribution: The program has slightly sub-optimal fatigue distribution, with leg days being tougher than shoulder and arm days. This may not be a major issue for beginners or individuals training with low volumes.

It's worth noting that everyone's body responds differently to different training programs. It's important to find a program that works best for you and your individual needs and goals.

Conclusion

The Six-day Arnold Split program offers a well-structured and effective training regimen for individuals looking to maximize hypertrophy. The program's focus on low volume and high frequency of training specific muscle groups allows for optimal muscle growth and strength gains. By following the recommended weekly setup and considering the pros and cons, you can tailor the program to your needs and achieve your dream physique.

Remember, consistency and proper nutrition are key components in achieving your fitness goals.
